I’m tucked away in the library, studying on my spring break (is it bad that I should enjoy this?) when lo and behold, what gem of wisdom should I come across? Dangling below the title to a book’s first chapter I find the following:

> ‘For those who wish to find answers, it is a real step forward even to ask the right questions.’\
> –Aristotle
